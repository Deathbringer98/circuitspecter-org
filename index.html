About the Studio

Circuit Specter Studio is an independent video game development studio focused on creating original, polished experiences. We're a small team driven by a big passion for games, design, and storytelling.

Right now, all of our energy is focused on our debut title, Asteroid Cats. We believe in building games we genuinely want to play — and sharing that journey with our players along the way.

Asteroid Cats is developed by Matthew M.

We're Looking for Volunteers!

Asteroid Cats is roughly 95% complete, and I've built every part of it myself. To add the final layer of polish, I'm looking for talented voice actors and artists who'd like to be part of a shipping indie game.

Voice Actors

Lend your voice to characters in Asteroid Cats and get real, credited work in a finished title for your reel.

Artists

Contribute artwork and visual polish to a nearly-complete game, and build your portfolio with shipped work.

Let's be upfront about it

This is currently a one-person studio, and I'm not in a position to pay contributors right now. In exchange for your time, I'll happily provide a professional reference to help you land future work — this is a real, nearly-finished project you can point to.

If someone brings genuine value and I really enjoy working with them, there's a chance I'll bring them on as a paid employee down the line — but I want to be honest: there are no guarantees. Please only volunteer if a credit and a reference feel worth it to you.
